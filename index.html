ABOUT US

Black Rose Studio is built on over twenty years of international tattooing experience.
We are home to experienced tattooers, all with complimentary skills, styles & artistic backgrounds.
Our aim is that our works speaks for it's self.

We believe in art.

We believe in creative expression, and we believe everyone has the right to appreciate it, on a wall, on a canvas, on skin.

We believe high-quality art is its own reward.

We believe in creating community around that art, a place where artists and art lovers can come together.

We believe the tattoo shop is one of those communities, and we welcome anyone who feels inspired by what we do.

We believe in humility. We are tattoo artists, not primadonnas.

ARTISTS

Morticia Adams

Morticia´s artwork is strongly influenced by pop culture. Her style reflects comic books, video games, cartoons, and film imagery are the things she likes to tattoo the most, and he always gets excited when he gets the chance to do something in that vein. As thoroughly as she enjoys making the weird, over-the-top tattoos, she always crafts the cleanest tattoo she can, regardless of style. Her favorite color is black.

Edward Scissorhands

Edward has a background in media arts, graphic design and animation. Specializing in Manga, anime, cartoon and comic style, he doesn’t shy away from fine lines, tiny details or realism. Edward has a preference for black and grey but is no stranger to color. His tattooing style is best described as traditional and romantic yet eccentric. He looks to the Renaissance for artistic inspiration and traditional American tattoo flash as a base. His style is influenced by artists such as Da Vinci, Rafaelo, Perugino, Hieronimus Bosh, Daci and Caravaggio, to name a few.
